Get Started

Welcome to Buzzby, made by Nihal Puram. It is a simple blogger, where users can create, and edit posts. You can view the latest news, and also specific news in the topics that interest you. You can view the current weather of all major centres across the world. To get started click Register, or login if you are a returning user. If you are already logged in click on Latest Posts.

Login or Register to view the latest posts

All your passwords are hashed and secure within the database. You can also set your profile picture. A desktop environment is preferred although mobile also works.

Be responsible, Have fun, and start posting. Feel free to reach out to Nihal Puram for any suggestions or feedback.
